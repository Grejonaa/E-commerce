Who We Are?

Welcome to Zara, a global leader in fashion design, manufacturing, and retail. Founded in 1974 in A Coruña, Spain, Zara is part of the Inditex Group, one of the world's largest and most innovative fashion retailers. With a commitment to delivering high-quality, trend-forward clothing, Zara has become a go-to destination for individuals who value style, functionality, and sustainability. With over 2,000 stores across 96 markets and a robust online presence, Zara remains committed to creating meaningful connections with our customers, offering a seamlessshopping experience that combines the best of technology, design, and sustainability.
